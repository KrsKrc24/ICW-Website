Event Timeline

Every event that has brought us closer to our mission — in reverse chronological order.

2026
February 2026
Holi Fundraising Booth
Community Event

ICW brought awareness and fundraising energy to the vibrant Holi celebration — combining a prize spinner, candy distribution, interactive activities, and education about pediatric cancer to engage the community and raise funds.

Community Engagement Awareness Booth
January 2026
Mammogram Van Tour
Swedish Medical Center · Seattle, WA

ICW partnered with Swedish Medical Center to sponsor a mobile mammogram van tour, bringing life-saving cancer screening directly to underserved communities across the Seattle area. A landmark collaboration with an established medical institution.

$1,000 Donated Swedish Medical Center
2025
October 2025
MOD Pizza Fundraiser
MOD Pizza · Sammamish, WA

Over 100 community supporters came out to MOD Pizza in Sammamish for a give-back night. A portion of all proceeds supported ICW's pediatric wellness and childhood cancer initiatives — proving local businesses and nonprofits can create real impact together.

100+ Supporters Restaurant Partnership
August 2025
Awareness Booth — Ananda Mela
Ananda Mela Festival · Pacific Northwest

ICW set up an educational awareness and fundraising booth at Ananda Mela — one of the largest annual celebrations of Indian culture in the United States. Through speech, communication, and interactive activities, the team reached hundreds of attendees and raised $800.

$800 Raised Hundreds Reached
June 2025
Handmade Cards — Seattle Children's Hospital
Seattle Children's Hospital · Seattle, WA

ICW volunteers gathered to create heartfelt, handwritten cards delivered directly to children undergoing cancer treatment. Because healing isn't only physical — every card carried a message that someone out there was thinking of them and rooting for their recovery.

Direct Patient Impact Seattle Children's Hospital
April 2025
Dance Charity Show — ISKCON
ISKCON Vedic Cultural Center

A beautiful evening of cultural dance performances at the ISKCON Vedic Cultural Center, drawing an enthusiastic crowd and raising $600 for childhood cancer detection and treatment programs. Attendance grew significantly compared to ICW's debut event.

$600 Raised Cultural Performance
January 2025
Handmade Cards — Bangalore, India
Sammprada Hospital · Bangalore, India

ICW's first international outreach event — a community card-making campaign for children at Sammprada Hospital in Bangalore. This milestone marked ICW's expansion beyond the US and established our international commitment to bringing compassion across borders.

First International Event Sammprada Hospital
2024
October 2024
Inaugural Dance Charity Show
Issaquah Community Center · Issaquah, WA

ICW's very first public event — a debut dance charity show that launched our mission to the world. The show raised $500 for early cancer detection and set the tone for everything ICW would become: community-driven, youth-powered, and deeply compassionate.

$500 Raised ICW's Founding Event
