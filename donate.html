Your Impact

Where Your
Money Goes

Care Packages & Cards

Funds handwritten cards and care packages delivered to children at Seattle Children's Hospital and Sammprada Hospital in Bangalore.

Hospital Donations

Direct funding to hospitals in India and partnerships with institutions like Swedish Medical Center for mobile screening programs.

Research & Advocacy

Supporting educational outreach, grant writing, and institutional partnerships that connect research with real-world care.

Chapter Programs

Enabling 21+ chapters across 4 continents to run local events, campaigns, and outreach that reach communities globally.
