Our Global Presence

A Movement That Knows No Borders

ICW's chapter model empowers students everywhere to take ownership of the fight for pediatric health equity. Each chapter is student-led and community-focused — bringing ICW's programs to their local hospitals, schools, and events.

Whether organizing care package drives, hosting fundraising events, or conducting educational outreach, every chapter is a force multiplier for our mission — and proof that young people anywhere can change everything.

Join the Network

Start a Chapter

You don't need experience — just passion. ICW gives you everything you need to bring our mission to your school or community.

1

Reach Out to ICW HQ

Email us at internationalchildrenwellness@gmail.com expressing your interest. Tell us about your school, community, and why you want to start a chapter.

2

Get Onboarded

HQ will provide you with the ICW chapter handbook, program guidelines, fundraising resources, and direct mentorship from existing chapter leaders.

3

Build Your Team

Recruit fellow students who share your passion. Chapters typically start with 4–6 members and grow from there — every person makes a difference.

4

Launch Your First Initiative

Start with a care package drive, awareness booth, or small fundraiser. HQ supports your first event every step of the way.

5

Join the Global Network

Your chapter becomes part of ICW's growing global community — connecting with peers on 4 continents working toward the same mission.

Ready to Start?

It takes one motivated student to change a community. Whether you're in Washington, Tampa, Ghana, or anywhere else — there's a place for you in ICW.
